Ponyo's Ramen

Description

This ramen is inspired by the warm, cozy moment in Ponyo on the Cliff when Sosuke’s mother prepares a comforting bowl of instant noodles topped with love (and ham). It’s simple, nourishing, and nostalgic—perfect for stormy nights, sleepy afternoons, or when the world feels just a bit too much. Like the film itself, this dish is a celebration of small joys, made with care and eaten quickly, before the noodles go soggy.

Ingredients

  • 1 instant ramen noodle packet (any kind you like)
  • 1 egg, hard or soft boiled
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 1 piece of ham, cut in half
  • 2.5 cups water

Steps

  1. Prep the ingredients: Hard or soft boil the egg and cut it in half. Slice the green onion and cut the ham into two pieces.
  2. Boil 2.5 cups of water in a kettle or on the stove.
  3. Place the ramen noodles into a bowl. If your ramen packet comes with a seasoning packet, open it and add it to the bowl now.
  4. Pour the boiling water into the bowl over the noodles. Cover the bowl and let it sit for 3–5 minutes, or according to the instructions on your ramen package.
  5. Once the noodles are cooked, uncover and gently separate them with chopsticks or a fork. Stir well to mix the seasoning into the broth.
  6. Top the ramen with the halved egg, sliced green onions, and both pieces of ham.
  7. Enjoy immediately while the noodles are hot and springy. Letting them sit too long will make them soggy!
